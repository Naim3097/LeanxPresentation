# Lean.x Presentation — Strategic Reframing

**Date:** 20 May 2026
**Owner:** Nexova Digital
**Audience flow:** Lean.x HODs → Lean.x Directors

---

## 1. The Real Story (Context)

The deck previously read like a fresh pitch. The truth is more nuanced and stronger — this is the **next chapter** of an already-working collaboration:

- **Month 1** — Existing engagement: **Brand Presence + Social Media Posts** for brand visibility and digital reach.
  - RM 5,500 / month, inclusive of RM 500–1,000 ads budget.
  - 15 social media posts/month, static visuals, ads-style graphic designs.
- **Month 2** — Lean.x requested **WhatsApp Conversation Ads** (Lean.x was ready to convert leads via WhatsApp).
  - Top-of-funnel was **collapsed and replanned straight into engagement → conversion**.
- **Month 3 → present** — Nexova Digital proposed building the **complete merchant acquisition ecosystem** (full `nexova.my` platform, SEO satellites, AI tooling, Lean.x Social, marketplace, etc.).
  - Built and shipped (17 weeks, 50+ phases). This is what the rest of the deck presents.

## 2. Decision: Make the Story Its Own Slide

The history is too important to bury in a subtitle. **A dedicated context slide is inserted at position 02** — between the Cover and the Stats slide.

- **Title:** "How we got here" / "Three months. Three evolutions of strategy."
- **Visual:** 3-step horizontal timeline (Month 1 → Month 2 → Month 3+)
- **Purpose:** Set the partnership context in 30 seconds before any technical content.

This makes the rest of the deck land as *"here's what Month 3 onwards delivered"* — not *"please consider this new proposal."*

## 3. The New Number: RM 15,000 / month

Previously RM 12,000. **Now RM 15,000.**

This is **NOT** framed as "continuing engagement + new ops." It is framed as: **the full monthly operating budget to run the ecosystem at maximum efficiency.** Every ringgit is allocated to either:

1. **Direct paid acquisition** (gets merchants on Lean.x fast)
2. **Compounding organic** (SEO + social — owned channels)
3. **Infrastructure & tooling** (keeps the engine running)
4. **Growth activation** (KOL + agency launch)

- **~10% growth activation (RM 1,500)** — KOL + agency launch.

## 4. The Strategic Promise

Sustainability + reach + conversion compounding:

1. **Organic** — SEO satellites + Lean.x Social + Nexova SEO build permanent inventory; the engine keeps running when paid spend pauses.
2. **Multi-angle** — Google intent, META social intent, TikTok founder content, WhatsApp conversation, KOL, plus owned `leanx.io` direct traffic. No single point of failure.
3. **Multi-target** — DIY merchants (Nexova Builder) + enterprise (Nexova Digital) + direct Lean.x merchants (Lean.x Social Media). Three buyer profiles, one gateway.
4. **Unique platform** — `nexova.my` is owned, not rented from Shopify/SaaS. The platform itself is the differentiator.
5. **Shortest acquisition points** — every component collapses the distance between traffic and a Lean.x transaction (native checkout, direct social CTAs, WhatsApp lead-to-payment).

## 6. Tone Guidelines

- **Direct, factual, business-meeting tone** — no marketing fluff.
- **The history slide tells the story once, properly.** Other slides do not need to repeat it.
- **No surprise framing** — "the combined monthly engagement is now RM 15,000" not "we are asking for RM 15,000".
- **Lead with what's already working** — WhatsApp Conversation Ads is the proof point of partnership rhythm.

## 7. What We Do NOT Change

- Visual design, layouts, fonts, colours
- Technical feature descriptions
- Glimsy live-proof slide (proven results)
- Ecosystem Map / Funnel / Four Layers strategic anchors
